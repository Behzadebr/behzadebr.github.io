About me...

I am Behzad Ebrahimi, a PhD student at the University of Southern California. I'm pursuing my PhD degree in Coastal Engineering at the department of civil and Environment Engineering, and my master's degree in Computer Science. As a PhD student, I am working as graduate research assistant, and graduate teaching assistant. In the research side, I'm involved in several different projects related to Coastal Engineering, Environment Engineering, and Computer Science. We recently published our paper in Nature, the most prestigious scientific journal, and have another article under review by Energy journal. On the teaching side, I am mostly in charge of teaching programming languages to undergraduate students. I was awarded as the best teaching assistant in 2021-22 academic year. I'm currently focusing on a multidisciplinary project, using game engines and advanced hydrodynamics to create a ship simulator for operational readiness in maritime and littoral operations(supported by the US Army).
